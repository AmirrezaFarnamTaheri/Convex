Detailed Curriculum

00. Linear Algebra Primer

Focus: Essential review: norms, inner products, eigenvalues, PSD matrices, quadratic forms. Start here if you need background.

01. Introduction: What & Why of Convex Optimization

Focus: Optimization landscape, convex vs nonconvex, examples in ML, control, and geometry.

02. Convex Sets: Affine, Cones, Balls, Ellipsoids

Focus: Geometric foundations: convex sets, operations preserving convexity, supporting hyperplanes.

03. Convex Functions: Characterization & Operations

Focus: Jensen, epigraphs, first/second-order tests, operations preserving convexity.

04. Convex Optimization Problems: Standard Forms

Focus: LP, QP, SDP, SOCP—how to recognize and formulate convex problems.

05. Duality: Lagrangian, KKT, Strong Duality

Focus: Dual problems, optimality conditions, shadow prices, and why duality powers algorithms.

06. Applications I: Approximation & Fitting

Focus: Least squares, robust regression, sparse recovery, regularization.

07. Applications II: Statistical Estimation & Machine Learning

Focus: MLE, logistic regression, SVM, classification—convex optimization at the heart of ML.

08. Applications III: Geometric Problems

Focus: MVEE, Chebyshev center, best-fit shapes, distance problems.

09. Algorithms I: Unconstrained Minimization

Focus: Gradient descent, Newton's method, step size selection, convergence rates.

10. Algorithms II: Equality-Constrained Minimization

Focus: Null-space methods, projected gradient descent, penalty methods.

11. Algorithms III: Interior-Point Methods

Focus: Barrier methods, central path, polynomial-time complexity, practical implementations.
