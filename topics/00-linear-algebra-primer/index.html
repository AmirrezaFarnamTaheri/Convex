00. Linear Algebra Primer

Date: 2025-10-14 · Duration: 60 min · Tags: prerequisites, review, linear-algebra

Overview: Essential review: norms, inner products, eigenvalues, PSD matrices, quadratic forms. Start here if you need background.

Prerequisites: None

Learning Objectives

After this lecture, you should understand:

  • Vector norms, inner products, and their geometric interpretations.
  • Eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and the properties of Positive Semidefinite (PSD) matrices.
  • The connection between quadratic forms and matrix properties.

Key Concepts

Concept 1: Norms and Inner Products

Detailed explanation with inline LaTeX: The inner product of two vectors is defined as $\langle x, y \rangle = x^T y$.

Visual intuition: The inner product relates to the angle between two vectors.

Concept 2: Eigenvalues and PSD Matrices

A matrix $A$ is PSD if $x^T A x \ge 0$ for all $x$.
