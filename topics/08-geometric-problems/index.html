Key Concepts

Concept 1: Distance Between Sets

The distance between two sets $\mathcal{C}_1$ and $\mathcal{C}_2$ is defined as $\inf\{\|x_1 - x_2\| \mid x_1 \in \mathcal{C}_1, x_2 \in \mathcal{C}_2\}$.

Visual intuition: We are finding the shortest line segment connecting the two sets.
