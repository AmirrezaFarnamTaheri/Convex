Key Concepts

Concept 1: The Barrier Method

The barrier method is an iterative method for solving inequality-constrained convex optimization problems. It works by converting the constrained problem into a series of unconstrained problems.

Visual intuition: We are adding a barrier to the objective function that prevents us from violating the constraints.
